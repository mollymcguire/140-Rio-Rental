Welcome to Rio Salado

Located in the highly desireable district of Tempe Town Lake, Rio Salado is nestled between Tempe Town Lake, 'A Mountain', ASU Stadium and the lightrail. Rio Salado sits within an urban neighborhoor that has grown into a vibrant, unique mid-city community that is everything you need and everything you want.

The unit consists of a gound-floor two bedroom, two bath open living floorplan, where the gourmet kitchen opens up into the family room. Together, this 'greatroom space' is surrounded with floor to ceiling windows that frame unobstructed, iconic Arizona views of Camelback Mountain, the Phoenix Preserve and Tempe Town Lake. The wrap-around patio provides an excellent venue to entertain family and friends as well as direct access to the community pool and Tempe Town Lake trail system.

It's here that you'll find the best of both worlds - private, resort-style accomodations in the heart of the city. Thoughtfully designed and luxuriously appointed, Rio Salado will feel and function like an extension of your home. It's here, where you can't help but relax and recharge.
